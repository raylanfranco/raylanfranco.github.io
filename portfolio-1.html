Metamorphosis

Application Objective:
Metamorphosis is a full-stack fitness tracker built to replace static spreadsheets with an intelligent, dynamic logging system. It leverages 1RM prediction formulas (Brzycki, Baechle, and Remedios), real-time workout logging, and mobile-optimized design to create a training companion tailored to serious lifters.
